Description

Our Artiste Range is the sensual, artisanal addition to our catalogue that is sure to find favour among those who demand aesthetic brilliance at an attractive price.

The Nettle Sofa Bed is a contemporary sofa, ideal for hotels, holiday lets & lodges. Part of our “EZ” Range, which means it’s easy to make into a relaxing double bed.

Available in a wide selection of faux leathers, fabrics or any other material (see below). The Dahlia is lovingly handmade and available in a wide range of options.

The Nettle comes with a plain backrest and plain seat, as per the photos, as standard. Should you wish to have a different material or style please contact our sales team who will be able to assist.

These items are lovingly made to order and must be purchased in a reasonable quantity. There is no minimum order on this item, however the more you buy, the cheaper the items and carriage will be on a pro-rata basis.

As these items are made to order you can customise this to suit your needs. Please call or email us if you have a bespoke request.

This item has a 5-6 week lead time and a 1 year warranty.

We suggest using ‘Panaz’ as standard for material. If you’d rather choose your own fabric please select from the materials below & call or email us. (COM – Customers Own Material).

Please click on the below links to view the fabric selection from Panaz:

– Panaz Marna Weave

– Panaz Luxe Velvet

– Panaz Aston Faux

We’d be happy to send you free material samples.

This item has a 1 year warranty. 

These items require some basic self assembly. 15LM Required per item of a plain material.

DIMENSIONS WHEN EXTENDED – 1450/1920mm
