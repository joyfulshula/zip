About Us

At its heart, ROCHALE LIMITED is a business that employs a dedicated and informed team who excel in providing the furniture for a whole variety of commercial environments & clients.

Our customers have included such luminaries as the Royal Albert Hall, The O2 Arena and the Savile Club while we are also proud to have provided renowned brands like McDonalds and Shakeaway with a range of stylish products.

From traditional to futuristic, from farmhouse chic to upmarket dining, ROCHALE LIMITED have been supplying high quality furniture and pride ourselves on exceptional service and keen pricing.

We understand that selling the product is only part of the dynamic between supplier and customer so we are proud to offer advice and dedicated support throughout your journey with us. We look forward to working together with you.
