The Merit Round Dining Table is a long-standing, budget friendly, easy to assemble and popular table for all manner of busy commercial venues. 

With the wide choice of colours, sizes and related products, the Merit Dining Range can suit your needs and wants. 

The Merit Table Range is made up of a 25mm Laminate Table Top with an MDF core and a matching 2mm ABS edge (unless said otherwise) & a square profile. The Merit table base is made from black cast iron.  Our laminate tops are made in the UK.

We also offer this table with a 18mm or 40mm thick top, 4-6 week lead time, POA.

This extremely durable item comes with height adjustable feet. Our laminate table tops can withstand heats up to 180 degrees centigrade, meaning there is no need for place mats or table protectors.

Laminate table tops can also withstand harsh cleaning products such as antibacterial cleaner. Our table bases come with a 2 year warranty and our table tops come with a 1 year warranty so you can have peace of mind for years to come. 

Some table tops may be textured, we’re happy to provide you with free samples should you require them.

This item requires basic self assembly. We offer this table range in Coffee, Dining and Poseur Heights as standard. We can cut these table bases to a custom height if you require, for a small extra cost.

Standard sizes are listed below.

*Easy Assembly is available as an optional extra – providing you with pre-drilled tops with metal inserts fitted in. Also comes with 8 x M6 screws that screw into these inserts. If you do not select this option, your tops will not come pre-drilled.*
